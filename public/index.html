Our Story

In 1996, a large, 3 foot balloon was filled with water and thrown it into a swimming pool, and the kids had a blast with it.

It was then, that the idea of water-inflated marine animals that would introduce children to an unlimited number of fun filled designs, was birthed. The idea was put on the back shelf until November 2014, when the inventor, John Newton, decided it was time to put his idea to the test.

That was when he began taking the steps to launch Pool-Pets.com LLC. Pool-Pets is the world's first water inflated pool toy. Instead of filling inflatables with air and having them float on top of the water, these are filled with water and glide "under" the water.

These are the Pool-Pets we have designed so far. Right now, only Opie the Orca and Stretch the Stingray are available.
